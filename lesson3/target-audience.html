Target Audience

The target audience of the peachtowncommerce.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Every commerce owner interested in joining a community where customers and businesses alike support each other.
  • Age: 18 to 100
  • Motivation: Provide access to valuable resources, discounts, and relationships that help businesses save money and market their products. Boost sales and significantly improve a local business' visibility and credibility.
